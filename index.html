About Us

About Saving Grace Primary

Saving Grace Nursery & Primary School is a Christian-founded school based in Lira, Uganda, committed to providing high-quality education to both street-connected and local community children. We focus on academic excellence, moral formation, and emotional healing.

What We Do

We rescue, restore, and reintegrate vulnerable children by offering education, spiritual guidance, trauma counseling, life skills training, and a nurturing environment where they can grow and thrive.

Academic Excellence

At Saving Grace, we deliver a strong academic curriculum taught by qualified and passionate teachers. Our learners are consistently among the top performers in the district, and we prepare them not just to pass exams, but to succeed in life with knowledge, skills, and confidence.
